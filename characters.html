My Favourite Characters

Introducing the characters that I enjoy!

Xiao


Xiao (Chinese: 魈 Xiāo, "Mountain Demon") is a playable Anemo character in Genshin Impact.

He is an adeptus, under the name Alatus, and the only known remaining member of the five foremost Yakshas dispatched by Morax to subdue the demonic spirits that plagued Liyue. He currently resides in Wangshu Inn and mostly secludes himself from crowds and human interactions.

I like to play using Xiao because his playstyle is super fun and overall, I think his character design is really pretty! He's also really good when you explore the open-world.

Kamisato Ayato


Kamisato Ayato (Japanese: 神里綾人) is the current head of the Kamisato Clan, the older brother of Kamisato Ayaka, and the Yashiro Commissioner.

Ayato's playstyle is very simple and easy. His character design also makes him unique since no other character in Inazuma dresses like him. Also, he's known to be cunning and very smart, which often leads to him working behind-the-scenes.

Click here for his video demonstration!


Arataki Itto


Arataki Itto (Japanese: 荒あら瀧たき一いっ斗と Arataki Itto) is a descendant of the crimson oni and also the leader and founder of the Arataki Gang.

For Itto, his personality is hilarious and very refreshing in Genshin Impact. I think he's one of my most favourite characters because of his easygoing self.

Click here for his video demonstration!


Diona Kätzlein


Despite being a bartender at the Cat's Tail, she actually despises alcohol and wants nothing more than to ruin Mondstadt's wine industry — a feat easier said than done when her patrons absolutely love her drinks. She is the daughter of Draff, a hunter in Springvale.

Diona is actually a very good character because she provides a relatively strong sheild as well as a good amount of healing for the characters. She's also very versatile and can be used in many team compositions!
